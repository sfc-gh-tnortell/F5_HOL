# F5 HOL - Sample Agent Questions

Use these to test the two agents built in Module 1 and Module 2.

## Support & Telemetry Agent (Module 1)

1. What telemetry signals are elevated for Tesla and do they have any matching open support cases?
2. Show me all accounts with bot transactions over 300K that also have open Bot Defense cases.
3. Which accounts have the most SLA breaches in the last 6 months?
4. What's the average resolution time for P1 cases by product?
5. Are there any accounts with high WAF usage but no open security cases?
6. Show me Tesla's open cases and tell me what their telemetry looks like - is their infrastructure under stress?
7. Which region has the most open P1 and P2 cases right now?
8. Find accounts where DNS telemetry is above 7 zones and show me their support history.
9. What products generate the most support load across all accounts?
10. Create a Salesforce case for AT&T noting their high load balancer utilization and recurring performance issues.

## Sales & Cross-Sell Agent (Module 2)

1. What should we be recommending to AT&T based on their current products and usage?
2. Show me the top 10 expansion opportunities across all accounts sorted by confidence.
3. Which accounts are candidates for AI Security products that don't already own them?
4. What's the total pipeline value for expansion deals currently in progress?
5. Show me accounts that own BIG-IP hardware but haven't bought any XC cloud products yet.
6. What are the highest confidence cross-sell recommendations - new product families we can introduce?
7. Which accounts have renewals coming up in the next 6 months with high utilization?
8. For my account [YOUR ACCOUNT], what's the revenue history and what do we recommend next?
9. Show me all accounts where we recommend capacity upgrades - they're over 80% utilization.
10. What's the win rate on expansion deals for accounts that already own NGINX products?

## Spanning Both Agents (Full Picture)

1. My account is Tesla. What are their open support issues, and based on their telemetry + product gaps, what should we pitch them at the next QBR?
2. Find accounts with high support case volume AND high-confidence cross-sell recommendations - these are accounts we need to stabilize before we sell more.
3. Which accounts have declining telemetry trends but we're still recommending they buy more? Should we be concerned?
4. Show me accounts where the support cases are about performance issues and we're recommending capacity upgrades - that validates the model is right.
5. What's the correlation between number of support cases and likelihood of buying expansion products?
